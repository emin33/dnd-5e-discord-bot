## Systemic theme (the thing the first pass structurally couldn't see)

**Player/world-state write-back is the weakest link, and it's split across three layers
that each assume another does the persisting.** The narrator's "authoritative" effect
executors (`_execute_update_player`, `_execute_change_location`) only *log* and return
`success=True`; the combat sync layer (`sync_to_character`) only copies a subset of fields;
and the streaming narration path can't even *emit* the tools that would trigger state
changes. Three agents independently hit facets of this. The result: trap damage, healing,
loot, currency, conditions applied outside combat, plus spell-slot/concentration spend and
temp-HP/death-save progress crossing the combat boundary, are silently dropped. This is the
single most important cluster in this audit and it is NEW.
